Moniker Marmot

It can be hard to pick a good name for something you've made. The difficulty is compounded when it needs to be reused by different stakeholders or incorporated into other projects. Names should be durable, inclusive, and descriptive without being too specific.

Moniker Marmot can help you choose a name by matching a descriptive word with an alliterative animal mascot. Create names for reusable projects, templates, and designs that have personality and style.
